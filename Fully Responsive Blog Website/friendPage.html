Meet my closest and dearest friend, Mr. Bikesh, also known as Sound. In my life, I have never been more grateful for anyone than I am for him. Let me share a story with you - when I first met him, I was in 8th grade and he was in 10th. At the time, I didn't know much about him, but as fate would have it, we both ended up studying in the same school and decided to share a room for studying purposes. At first, I thought he was just like any other boy, but after living with him for a few weeks, I became a huge fan of his. Our thoughts and perspectives were so aligned, and we had such a great time together for two whole years. Those were some of the best moments of my life and I wish I could go back and relive them. I can't even begin to describe in words how incredible those days were when we were together. Unfortunately, now we're far apart from each other and it truly sucks. If I keep writing, this will end up being a book with thousands of pages. I miss you every day, bro.
